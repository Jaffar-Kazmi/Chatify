# Privacy Policy for Chatify

**Effective Date:** December 20, 2025

Chatify (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application Chatify.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Chatify, we may collect the following information:

- **Account Information:**  
  - Email address, username, and password to create and authenticate your account.

- **Messages and Media:**  
  - Text messages and images sent through the app.

- **Device Information:**  
  - Device type, operating system, app version, and IP address for performance monitoring and troubleshooting.

- **Usage Data:**  
  - How you interact with the app, including feature usage, chat activity, and app navigation.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

- To provide, operate, and maintain Chatify.
- To facilitate real-time messaging between users.
- To improve and personalize your experience.
- To monitor app performance and fix issues.
- To communicate important updates, promotions, or security alerts.
- To comply with legal obligations.

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell or rent your personal information**. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers:**  
  Third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., cloud storage, analytics). They are obligated to protect your data.

- **Legal Compliance:**  
  If required by law or to protect our rights, we may disclose information to law enforcement or other authorities.

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.  

**Note:** No method of data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

- **Access and Update Your Account Information:**  
  Edit your profile information or change your password.

- **Delete Your Account:**  
  You can request to delete your account, which will remove most of your data from our servers.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Chatify is **not intended for children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with a **new effective date**. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
